What to Do After Receiving an Eviction Notice

If you’ve received an eviction notice, it’s normal to feel anxious. Taking calm, organized steps early can make a difference.

Common first steps tenants take

  1. Read the notice carefully and note all dates
  2. Make copies or photos of the notice
  3. Identify the type of notice
  4. Organize lease and payment records

Avoid common mistakes

  • Ignoring the notice
  • Missing deadlines
  • Relying on assumptions or rumors

Prepare for what may come next

Even if court is not mentioned yet, preparation helps. Knowing the process reduces panic and confusion.
