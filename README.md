# Eviction Defense AI

Eviction Defense AI is a public, educational self-help tool designed to help tenants understand eviction notices, timelines, and common response options.

This project provides **plain-language guidance** to help individuals facing eviction organize next steps and avoid missing critical deadlines.

> This tool is for educational purposes only and does not provide legal advice or legal representation.

---

## What This Tool Does

- Explains common eviction notice types
- Outlines the general eviction process
- Helps tenants identify where they are in the process
- Shows common response paths and next steps
- Connects users to optional self-help document resources

---

## Who This Is For

- Renters who received an eviction notice
- Tenants with an upcoming court date
- Individuals unsure what an eviction notice means
- People seeking affordable self-help resources

---

## What This Tool Is NOT

- Not a law firm
- Not a legal service provider
- Not an attorney
- Not legal advice

This project is designed to **educate and organize**, not replace licensed legal professionals.
